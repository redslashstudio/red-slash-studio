Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 March 2026

Who we are

Red Slash Studio Limited (company number 13060979) operates redslashstudio.com. When this policy says "we" or "us," it means Red Slash Studio Limited.

What we collect

When you use the contact form on this site, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message

We don't use cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts on this site.

How we use it

We use the information you submit through the contact form solely to respond to your enquiry. We won't add you to a mailing list, share your details with third parties, or use your information for marketing purposes.

How it's processed

Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree, which forwards your message to us via email. Formspree's own privacy policy governs how they handle data in transit.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. When you visit, GitHub's servers process your IP address and may collect basic technical information such as your browser type. We don't control this processing. See GitHub's privacy statement for details.

Fonts

This site loads typefaces from Google Fonts. When a page loads, your browser makes a request to Google's servers, which means Google receives your IP address. See Google's privacy policy for details on how they handle this data.

How long we keep it

We retain contact form submissions only as long as needed to respond to your enquiry and for any resulting project discussions. If nothing comes of it, we delete the correspondence.

Your rights

You can ask us to delete any data we hold about you, or to confirm what we have, by emailing hello@redslashstudio.com.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
